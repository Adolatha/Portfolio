ABOUT My approach • focus areas • mindset

Cybersecurity across the full stack, from systems and networks to risk and compliance.

I explore how systems fail, how attackers operate, and how organisations can build resilient security practices. I work across technical security (infrastructure, testing, threat intelligence) and cyber risk assessment and governance.

I don’t treat cybersecurity as “one role.” I treat it as an ecosystem: prevention, detection, response, and governance—because strong security is what still holds when things go wrong.
