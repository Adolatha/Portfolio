About Me

Who I Am

I approach cybersecurity as both a technical and strategic discipline — one that connects systems, risks, behaviour, and resilience.

ABOUT Focus areas • mindset • approach

Cybersecurity across systems, infrastructure, applications, and secure thinking.

I am studying Applied Computer Science with a specialization in Cybersecurity. I enjoy understanding how systems work, how they fail, and how they can be made more resilient. My interests span practical security engineering, network and infrastructure security, threat intelligence, secure development, and analysis of malicious behaviour.

I do not see cybersecurity as a single isolated role. I see it as an ecosystem of prevention, detection, analysis, and continuous improvement. That mindset shapes how I learn, how I build projects, and how I reflect on security challenges.

Whether I am working on honeypot ideas, secure application design, or event reflections, I care about making security understandable, practical, and useful.
